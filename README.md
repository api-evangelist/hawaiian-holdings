# Hawaiian Holdings (hawaiian-holdings)

Hawaiian Holdings was the parent company of Hawaiian Airlines, the largest and longest-serving airline of Hawaii, providing scheduled passenger service to destinations across the Pacific. In 2024 the company combined with Alaska Air Group, and Hawaiian Holdings became a wholly-owned subsidiary of Alaska Air Group. No public developer portal or production-facing APIs are advertised.
